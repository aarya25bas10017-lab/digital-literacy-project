
Stay Safe Online — Prevention Checklist
Do not click on suspicious or unknown links received via email or messages
Always verify the sender’s email address before responding
Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
Never share OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone
UPI / Financial Safety:
Do not approve unknown UPI collect requests
Never scan QR codes sent by unknown persons for receiving money
Always verify payment requests before completing transactions
General Safety:
Use only secure websites (https://) for transactions
Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or sensitive activities
Regularly monitor bank statements and account activity
Report Cybercrime:
National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
Helpline Number: 1930
